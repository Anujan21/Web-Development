ABOUT US

About Cosmic Universe

At Cosmic Universe, our goal is to provide both interesting and valuable information to those who are interested in space, and to spark interest to those who don't know much about space. We believe there is content on this website for all kinds of users, and we try our best to provide the most accurate and reliable information we can find. We hope that users can feel like their time on our site was worthwhile, whether it was learning more about something they found interesting, or just getting a general idea of a topic for the first time.

Motivation

Some people ask "What's the point in learning about space?" Because they think that it might not matter to the average person. We have not just one reason that drives us but two. The first one being that we get to do what we love, and that is continuously learning about the objects and their behaviours in space, which is what inspires us and feels meaningful to research, learn and teach the things that we find interesting. The second reason is that most people aren't aware of the possible threats that may be beyond the atmosphere of our own planet. By learning about what's our there, we can better equip and protect our own planet from another possible asteroid, or a different kind of threat that no one has ever seen before.
